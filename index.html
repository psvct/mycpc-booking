Expert Q&A

Everything you need to know

Can I drive while waiting for my new card?
You must not drive professionally without a valid CPC card. However, if your card is delayed after completion, you can drive for up to 15 days if you carry proof of your completed hours and have applied for the card.
What software do I need to join?
We use Google Meet. It's accessible via any modern browser on phones, tablets, or laptops. No special installation is required—just click the link we email you.
Are you an approved center?
Yes. We are JAUPT Approved Centre AC02237. All our courses are fully accredited and count towards your 35-hour periodic training requirement.
